The Artist

I am based in the beautiful Tamar Valley, where I draw inspiration from the nature that surrounds me. Within my artwork, you will find hares, bees, flowers, rolling hills and usually a cat or 5. I am also love and am very heavily influenced by pattern and colour.

You will find me ensconced in my studio, surrounded by inks, lino, cutting tools, a huge mug of tea and usually a couple of fat, lazy cats planting themselves where they really shouldn’t be planted!
